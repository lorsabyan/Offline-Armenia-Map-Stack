Interactions
Type in the search bar to find places, streets and addresses
Click Route, then type or click two points to get driving directions. Drag markers to adjust.
Right-click anywhere to snap to the nearest object — building, shop, crossroad or street
Click coordinates in the status bar or the Copy button in popups to copy to clipboard
Snap rules (right-click)
Named places (shops, cafes, monuments…) — highest priority, 8 m bonus
Crossroads — where 2+ named roads meet within 35 m, snaps to centre
Addresses (house numbers) — 3 m bonus over plain roads
Roads — fallback, snaps to nearest point on the road network
A dashed line shows the offset from your click to the snapped point
